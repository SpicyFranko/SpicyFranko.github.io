Shopping

While Jerome might be an old mine town there is plenty of shops and restraints that are available for tourist to make a stop by.

Top 3 Stores

Twisted Alchemist

A store that sells various crystals, rocks, and much more. They also offer hand made options such as wood crafts and products.

Laughing Mountain

A store that sells antiques, clothing, mugs, and other clothing items.

Mooey Christmas and Udder Things

Wish it was christmas everyday, well this store in particular offers all sorts of christmas themes items all year round, these include knick knacks, trees, globes and all sorts of stuff.

Top 3 Restaurants

Haunted Hamburger

A burger restaurant with a supposedly haunted venue.

Bobby D's BBQ

A BBQ restaurant that used to be a Asian restaurant that belonged to one man that had died on one of the tables making it haunted supposedly.

The Bordello of Jerome

A restaurant that offers food and booze hence that being their motto. Aside from that they offer various salads and burgers.
